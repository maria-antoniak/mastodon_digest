@alec @trisweb @mekkaokereke I took up weightlifting last year following Liftoff: Couch to Barbell. I had never done any kind of weightlifting before, and I love doing it now for exercise. Others' experience may vary, for sure, but I found the program easy to follow, and I appreciated Casey Johnston's gentle, thoughtful approach to health and fitness. I found weightlifting made a noticeable impact in my ability to move in my daily life, and I developed the confidence to add in more weightlifting exercises beyond what her program includes. I would definitely recommend it to someone looking to start weightlifting without any prior experience.

I got up to lifting 115 lbs (52 kg) from nothing on my squat and benchpress, and I could do 95 lbs (43 kg )with my deadlift and overhead press. I fell out of weightlifting consistently because my wife had our second child, and caring for a baby takes a ton of time and energy. Even with not going consistently to lift for almost a year now I can still lift 65 lbs (29 kg) OK.

One of my favorite things about weightlifting, though, is that I can still lift up and carry my oldest child, who is 6 and weights around 47 lbs (21 kg). When I carry her and the baby together (about 65 lbs (29 kg) all together) they both get so tickled and laugh together!
